Topic:

Margaretha Haglund

Tobacco Control Sweden

Margaretha Haglund is a recognized expert in tobacco control both in Sweden and globally. She has a broad expertise in tobacco control in particular women and tobacco. In between 1979-1992 she was the head of tobacco control at the National Board of Health and Welfare. 1992-2011 she had the same position in the Institute of Public Health incl was a senior adviser to the D.G. Since 2011 she serves as an expert r at ThinkTank Tobaksfakta (an independant NGO) in Sweden. Margaretha is also the past president of the International Network of Women Against Tobacco (INWAT). She has also now and then a temporary expert to the WHO Europe. Latest project the publication; Empower Women.Facing the Challenge of Women and Tobacco in Europe. In 1994 she received the May 31 st WHO medal and in 2006 the prestigious Luther Terry Award.
